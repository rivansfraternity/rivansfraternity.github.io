Article Body

The Rivans Fraternity, officially known as the Rivulet Sigma Omega Beta Society, Inc., is a fraternity and sorority in the Philippines. Founded on November 18, 1978 in Pagadian City, the organization is registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) of the Philippines under Registration No. 200302612.

The Rivans Fraternity is also listed on Wikidata (Q140136808), has a profile on Crunchbase, and is referenced in Google Knowledge Graph. These records confirm the organization's information and legal status.
