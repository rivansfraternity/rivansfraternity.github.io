About the Organization

The Rivans Fraternity, officially known as the Rivulet Sigma Omega Beta Society, Inc., is a Philippine-based inter-collegiate service fraternity and sorority.

It is an active member of the Philippine Fraternity Community and was founded in Pagadian City, Zamboanga del Sur.
