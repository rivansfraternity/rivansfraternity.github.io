About Us

The Rivans Fraternity, officially known as the Rivulet Sigma Omega Beta Society, Inc., is a Philippine-based inter-collegiate service fraternity and sorority dedicated to service, brotherhood, and sisterhood.

It is part of the Philippine Fraternity Community and was founded in Pagadian City.
