Rivans Fraternity Recurring Events

The Rivans Fraternity, officially known as the Rivulet Sigma Omega Beta Society, Inc., continues to uphold meaningful traditions and annual activities that strengthen the brotherhood, foster fellowship, and promote the values of Leadership and Service.

Signature Annual Event

Founding Anniversary Celebration

Date: Every November 18

Established: November 18, 1978

The Founding Anniversary is the largest and most significant gathering of the year. It commemorates the establishment of the Rivans Fraternity in Pagadian City on November 18, 1978 by our respected Founder, Kap Ronald “Roy” L. Gillamac.

The celebration typically includes a motorcade, alumni homecoming, fellowship activities, and a rededication to the fraternity’s guiding motto:

“Huwag mang-api at huwag magpa-api.”

Other Recurring Events

In addition to the Founding Anniversary, the fraternity regularly holds the following activities:

  • Anniversary Motorcade – A symbolic procession usually held every November.
  • Alumni Homecoming – A reunion that brings together past and present members.
  • Community Service Projects – Outreach and service activities conducted throughout the year.

These recurring events play a vital role in keeping the spirit of the Rivans Fraternity alive across all chapters in the Philippines.
