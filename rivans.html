About the Rivans

The Rivulet Sigma Omega Beta Society, commonly known as the Rivans, is a service-oriented fraternity and sorority in the Philippines. Founded on November 18, 1978 in Pagadian City, the organization is officially registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC).

Principles and Objectives

Guided by belief in Divine Providence, the society is committed to developing reputable and progressive lives for its members through:

  • Genuine brotherhood and sisterhood
  • Helping strangers and those in need
  • Student development and community service

Member Identification

Members are identified by two dots on the left side of the wrist near the heart, symbolizing the fraternity and sorority.

Community Service

The Rivans actively participate in community development projects and volunteer initiatives, embodying the spirit of service and unity.
