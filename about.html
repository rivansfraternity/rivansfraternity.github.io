About Rivans Fraternity

This is the official organizational profile for Rivans Fraternity, serving as the digital foundation for our historic movement and fellowship.

Officially known as the Rivulet Sigma Omega Beta Society, we are a Philippine-based inter-collegiate service fraternity and sorority. Founded on November 18, 1978, in Pagadian City by our esteemed Founder, Kap Ronald "Roy" L. Gillamac, our organization proudly champions the core principles of Brotherhood, Service, and Leadership.

Driven by our official motto, “Huwag mang-api at huwag magpa-api,” every member proudly identifies as a "Solido Rivano" dedicated to lifelong fellowship and community service.
