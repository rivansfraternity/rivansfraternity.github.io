# rivansfraternity.github.io
The Rivans Fraternity, officially known as the Rivulet Sigma Omega Beta Society, is a Philippine-based inter-collegiate service fraternity and sorority. Founded on November 18, 1978, in Pagadian City by our esteemed Founder, Kap Ronald "Roy" L. Gillamac, our organization champions the core principles of Brotherhood, Service, and Leadership.
